# Map Legend

The player sees a hex-grid map in their UI. When they ask about colors or icons, use this reference.

## Sector Colors

- **Your current sector** — dark blue fill
- **Visited sector** — color filled with region color
- **Corp-visited sector** — same colors but with a dotted/dashed border (visited by a corporation ship, not you personally)
- **Unvisited sector** — gray, muted
- **Mega-port sector** — white glow effect
- **Garrison sector** — filled red if not your or your corporation

## Regions

- **Federation Space** — filled teal
- **Neutral Space** — fill indigo

## Icons

- **Port** — white swap/arrows icon (two arrows pointing opposite directions)
- **Mega-port** — star icon
- **Garrison** — castle/turret icon
- **Ship count** — badge with number

## Lanes (Connections Between Sectors)

- **Two-way warp** — solid line (no arrow)
- **One-way warp** — solid line with a directional arrow
